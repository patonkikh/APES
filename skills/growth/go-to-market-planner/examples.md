# Go-to-Market Planner — Examples

---

## Example 1: B2B AI writing assistant (PLG)

### Input

```text
MVP live: Gmail/Outlook plugin, SMB sales teams US.
Value prop: drafts replies in your tone from CRM context.
Budget: $50k launch quarter. Beta in 6 weeks.
```

### Output (excerpt)

```markdown
## Beachhead ICP
SMB sales teams 10–50 reps, US, HubSpot/Salesforce users.

## GTM motion
Product-led with sales assist for teams >30 seats.

## Channel plan
| Channel | Stage | KPI |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Product Hunt + content | Awareness | Signups |
| LinkedIn creators | Acquisition | Activated trials |
| HubSpot marketplace | Activation | Installs |

## Phase Beta exit criteria
40% users send 1+ AI draft in week 1.
```
